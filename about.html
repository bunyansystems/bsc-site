Our Story

Bunyan Systems & Consulting was founded to bring clean, scalable technology to small businesses in Cape Breton and beyond. We help service-based operations bridge the digital gap — without overcomplication or inflated cost.

Founder Neil Bunyan combines a decade of field-level quality control experience with a formal background in IT and cybersecurity. His deep understanding of real-world operations and backend systems enables Bunyan Systems to deliver impactful solutions that align with how your business actually works.

Mission & Values

Our mission is to empower growing businesses through practical digital systems, better processes, and modern tools. We're committed to:

  • • Simplifying systems without dumbing them down
  • • Serving local businesses with global-level expertise
  • • Prioritizing clarity, integrity, and sustainable growth
